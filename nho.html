Nhớ

[ɲɤ˧˥]

A complex feeling of missing someone
or something, mixed with memory,
longing, and emotional attachment.
It is stronger than simply “to remember”.

Living abroad, she often felt Nhớ for her hometown.
해외에 살면서, 그녀는 종종 고향을 향한 Nhớ를 느끼곤 했다.
En vivant à l'étranger, elle ressentait souvent
un Nhớ pour sa ville natale.
