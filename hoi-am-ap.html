Hơi ấm áp

[həːj˧˧ ʔəəm˧˥ ʔaːp̚˧]

“A little warm / slightly warm” describes
a gentle, pleasant warmth that feels
comfortable, cozy, and softly
affectionate rather than intensely hot.

The room felt warm, just Hơi ấm áp cozy.
방은 따뜻했고, Hơi ấm áp 아늑한 느낌이었다.
La chambre était chaleureuse, Hơi ấm áp cosy.
