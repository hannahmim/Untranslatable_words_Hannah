Giàu tình cảm

[zaw˨˩ tiŋ˧˨˩ kam˧˩˧]

Literally “rich in feelings.”
Describes someone who is emotionally
expressive, affectionate, and deeply sensitive.

He is Giàu tình cảm, always showing
care through small gestures.
그는 Giàu tình cảm하며, 작은 행동들로 늘 마음을 표현한다.
Il est Giàu tình cảm, montrant toujours
son attention à travers de petits gestes.
