Đông vui

[ɗoŋ˧ vuːj˧]

A lively atmosphere created by many
people gathering together in warmth and
celebration. It implies not just “crowded,” but
socially joyful and full of energy.

The market was Đông vui during the festival.
축제 기간 동안 시장은 Đông vui 했다.
Pendant le festival, le marché était Đông vui.
